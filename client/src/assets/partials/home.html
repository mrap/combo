What is Zen Typing?
It's all about flowing through character sequences.
As a Zen Typist, you'll experience unparalleled consistency, quickness, and confidence as you flow from sequence to sequence.
How do I learn?
The program trains you to "muscle-memorize" specific key combinations. These key combos are derived from the top 10000 most commonly used English words.
Master them by completing all levels.
Anything else?
Do yourself a favor - don't look at the keyboard under any circumstances while training. Take breaks every 20 minutes as it helps memorization.
